airless ball


The invisiball is not a basketball like any other. It doesn't need to be inflated and features a 3D printed polymer lattice structure that meets the performance specifications of a regulation basketball in terms of weight, size, and bounce. But it doesn't just mimic the performance of a traditional basketball, it redefines it. This ball promises an unprecedented playing experience, with engineering that seems to come from another planet.The innovative design of the balloon includes 8 panel-like lobes and a stitched structure, enhanced with hexagonal holes to allow free passage of air. Each Airless Gen1 comes in a premium pelican case, complete with a mirrored display stand and a custom 3D printed label.
